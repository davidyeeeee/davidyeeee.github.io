This section documents my exploration of AI-assisted creative writing, focusing on prompt engineering and narrative development using LLMs.

Creative Writing with AI

This project involved using DeepSeek's text generation model to create a two-chapter short story exploring teenage relationships and unspoken emotions. The experience provided valuable insights into AI-assisted creative writing and prompt engineering techniques.

Story Overview

The narrative follows Ethan and Clara, two high school students with mutual but unspoken feelings for each other. Ethan attempts to use their school play as a vehicle to express his emotions, while Clara withdraws from the leading role to create distance.

Through the play's dialogue—a counseling session between characters—they indirectly communicate their fears about the future and the inevitable separation that awaits them after graduation.

Themes: Unspoken emotions, teenage relationships, fear of the future, communication through art

Prompt Engineering Approach

The successful generation of this story relied on carefully structured prompts that included:

  • Clear structural requirements (two chapters, 300-500 words each)
  • Specific thematic elements (undefined teenage feelings, mature perspective)
  • Detailed character perspectives and narrative arcs
  • Contextual setting (school play rehearsal and performance)
  • Emotional tone and vocabulary specifications

The prompt effectively guided the AI to create a coherent narrative with distinct character voices and emotional depth.
